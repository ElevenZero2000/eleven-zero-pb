What we collect

Account details

When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, password hash, and basic profile information needed to operate the site.

Marketplace and directory content

When you publish a paddle listing, trainer profile, review, or court report, we collect the information you choose to submit, including pricing, location details, descriptions, ratings, skill levels, and availability notes.

Payments and payouts

If payments or seller payouts are enabled, Stripe processes payment and connected-account information. Eleven Zero PB may receive limited status, transaction, and verification data from Stripe so we can support checkout, seller onboarding, and payout readiness.

Usage and device information

We may collect analytics, page activity, browser type, referral data, IP-based location signals, and similar technical information to improve performance, understand traffic, and protect the platform.

How we use it

Operate the website

  • Create and secure accounts.
  • Show live listings, trainer profiles, reviews, and court details.
  • Support checkout, seller onboarding, and account dashboards.
  • Respond to support requests and service issues.

Improve trust and safety

  • Reduce spam, fraud, and abuse.
  • Review suspicious listings, reviews, or reports.
  • Investigate disputes, bugs, and misuse.

Measure growth

We may use analytics tools, including Google Analytics when enabled, to understand which pages are useful, how people discover the site, and where the experience can improve.

Legal and business reasons

We may use information as needed to comply with applicable law, enforce our terms, protect our rights, and maintain accurate records.

When we share information

Service providers

We may share data with providers that help run the site, such as hosting, payments, analytics, email, or automation tools, but only for business purposes tied to the platform.

Public content

Listings, trainer profiles, reviews, and court reports are intended to be visible to other users. Please post thoughtfully.

Legal requests and protection

We may disclose information if required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect users, the business, or the platform from harm or misuse.

Your choices

Account updates and deletion

You can contact us to update or remove account-linked information, listings, reviews, or trainer content. Some records may be retained for security, tax, payment, or legal reasons.

Community accuracy

Court details, crowd reports, player levels, and trainer information may come from user submissions or public map data. We try to keep the experience useful, but you should still confirm details before traveling, booking, or buying.

Policy changes

We may update this policy as Eleven Zero PB grows. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and post the revised version on this page.

Eleven Zero PB is building a better place to buy paddles, find courts, and connect with trainers while staying clear about how player data is used.
