How it works

Buying or selling a paddle should feel straightforward from the start.

Buyers should know what they can review before paying. Sellers should know what happens before their listing goes public. That clarity is the point.

1

Search with real filters

Buyers can narrow the shop by brand, thickness, color, condition, location, price, and more instead of digging through random posts.

2

Open the full listing

3

Submit, review, then publish

Sellers build the listing first, then it is reviewed before it appears in the public marketplace.
